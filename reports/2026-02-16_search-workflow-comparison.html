Free tier = Approach 3 (Parallel Merge) — CMS backbone + Google Places org resolution. Shows every provider with Medicare data, practice group grouping, and Google ratings where matched. Fast, cheap, dramatically better than Places-only search. This alone is more useful than anything on the market for liaisons.

Pro tier = Approach 4 (Full Intelligence) — adds web-extracted insurance panels, languages, "accepting patients," cross-location practice group mapping, and LLM-generated intelligence summaries. Pre-computed for common searches, on-demand for the rest. This is the moat.

The key realization from this data: Google Places returned 20 results with 17 orgs and 3 named doctors. CMS has 257 providers at those same addresses. The value isn't in either source alone — it's in the entity resolution that unpacks orgs into individuals and connects them across sources.
